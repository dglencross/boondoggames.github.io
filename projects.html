War Game [project name]

War Game [project name] is a dinosaur-themed strategy game in a similar vein to Archipelago.

The object of the game is to take over the territory of your opponents, which you do by launching dinosaurs at them. These dinosaurs travel by land, air and sea.

Currently it is a single-player game, but a multi-player version is in the pipeline!
